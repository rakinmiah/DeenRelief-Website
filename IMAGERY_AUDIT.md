# Imagery audit — DeenReliefMedia → website

Audit reviewing every new asset in `DeenReliefMedia/` against current website imagery usage, with specific recommendations for which campaign pages they belong on. The goal is to reduce repetition, increase relevance per cause page, and add imagery to under-illustrated sections.

Generated 2026-05-08. Mirror in `/public/images/` referenced where applicable.

---

## How to read this document

- **Section 1** lists the new images in `DeenReliefMedia/` that I've identified content for, grouped by best-fit campaign page.
- **Section 2** lists current website images by usage frequency — flags repetition.
- **Section 3** flags low-quality / dated / unused assets for cleanup or replacement.
- **Section 4** identifies pages lacking imagery and recommends additions.
- **Section 5** is a final do-this-first shortlist for the next imagery commit.

Filenames in DeenReliefMedia use Instagram archive prefixes (e.g. `deenrelief_*_n.jpg`, `anonyig.io_Instagram_*`, `SnapInsta.to_*`) — they're meaningless for content but stable identifiers.
